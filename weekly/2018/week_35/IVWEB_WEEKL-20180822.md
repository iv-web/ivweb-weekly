<h3>A. Trial/Onboarding</h3>

<p>In the <em>Trial</em> phase, our user is going to sign up and see if it is the product for them.</p>

<p>Joshua Porter sums it up:</p>

<blockquote>â€œOnboarding should not be a separate function/consideration/afterthought. It should be an initial (and primary) focus of design.â€<br /><br />&mdash; </blockquote>
